# Customers-Shopping-Behaviour-Dashboard

## Project Objective
The Shopping Behavior dataset provides insights into how customers purchase products across various categories such as Accessories, Clothing, Footwear, and Outerwear. It captures preferences based on color choices, seasonal trends, and gender-based buying patterns. Customers often prefer bright and trendy colours during festive or summer seasons.

## Dataset Used
<a href="https://github.com/Aravindkishore16/Shopping-Behaviour-Of-Customers/blob/main/SHOPPING%20BEHAVIOUR.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Questions(KPIs)

-Which colors are most preferred by customers across different product categories?

-Do color preferences change by season or gender?

-Which product category has the highest number of purchases?

-How do sales vary among accessories, clothing, footwear, and outerwear?

-Which season records the highest number of purchases?

-What are the top-selling product categories for male and female customers?

-Are there differences in color preferences between genders?

Dashboard Interation <a href="https://github.com/Aravindkishore16/Shopping-Behaviour-Of-Customers/blob/main/Screenshot%20(93).png">View Dashboard</a>

## Process

-Import and clean the shopping dataset to ensure accuracy in colour, category, season, and gender fields.

-Create pivot tables to analyze customer purchases by colour, category, season, and gender.

-Calculate total items purchased and revenue for each dimension to identify buying trends.

-Visualize insights using bar charts, pie charts, and seasonal trend lines.

-Combine all visuals into an interactive Excel dashboard with slicers for colour, season, and gender filters.

## Project Insights

-Clothing and Accessories are the most purchased categories, showing high customer engagement and demand.

-Male customers are dominating female customers in purchasing.

-Seasonal trends reveal that Spring and Fall season drive higher sales volumes across all categories.

## Final Conclusion

The analysis reveals strong customer preference for clothing and Accessories, making them key revenue drivers.Female shoppers show higher purchase frequency in fashion-related categories, while male shoppers favor outerwear and functional items.Seasonal demand significantly influences buying patterns, highlighting the need for timely stock management.Overall, understanding customer preferences by color, category, season, and gender can help retailers plan targeted marketing and boost sales performance.
